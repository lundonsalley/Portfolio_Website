This piece is a free write brainstorm of what topics I wanted to do my research paper on. I ended up not choosing any of these ideas, and instead opted to write about thrust vector control in rockets. Although none of these were used, it was still very important to my decision making process.



I chose to include this piece because I feel it shows some of the process I go through to create my writing. I normally cannot just start writing without some sort of brainstorming or thought-dumping.



This piece reflects my work in this class by showing my thinking before writing. I previously never spent much time planning for a writing piece, and just started writing things down when I needed to turn it in. As I've matured and grown, I've started to put more effort and thought into my writing to make it the best that I can.

What topics are you considering?

 

I would like to research either stealth technology or how different planets affect robots.

I feel like the stealth technology would be hard to research, as most of it is probably classified by the DoD, but I think the research on how to deal with environments on other planets and still allow robots to function correctly would be interesting. For example, Mars' lack of atmosphere leads to more radiation, and I wonder if it also correlates to more bits to be flipped by cosmic rays because of that lack of protection.
