PROJECT PORTFOLIO ANANKE

Hohmann Transfer in MATLAB

This program generates an animation of a Hohmann Transfer from Low Earth Orbit (LEO) to the moon. I hadn't taken Space Mechanics yet, so all of the physics used in the program was learned through an online Stanford powerpoint. Even without formally knowing the equations, I was able to get my values within a resonably small margin of error.
