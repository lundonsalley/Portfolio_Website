Team 6016 Ironwolves of Misson Vista

The Ironwolves are Mission Vista High School's robotics team that competes in the First Tech Challenge (FTC). Each year, teams from around the world are assigned a challenge, and they must design, build, test, and compete with their robot against other teams. The tasks in the challenges can range from lifting a cone onto a pole to making the entire robot do a 'pull-up' off the ground. More info on FTC can be found here.
